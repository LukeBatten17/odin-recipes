Breakfast Spaghetti

This is the famous breakfast of champions. Buddys favorite food

Ingredients

  • 1 Serving Spaghetti
  • Red and green M&M's
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Sprinkles
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Maple Syrup
  • Pop tart

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti according to the instructions on the box, about 9-10 minutes. Drain it in a colander and plate it.
  2. Add M&M's (optimally red and green ones), mini marshmallows, and rainbow sprinkles.
  3. Drizzle on chocolate syrup and maple syrup.
  4. Toast a chocolate fudge Pop Tart for toast.
  5. Enjoy the sugar rush
